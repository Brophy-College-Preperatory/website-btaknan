In-N-Out

In N Out Burger is an exclusive fast food restuarant to some west coast states such as; California, Colorado, Arizona, Nevada, Idaho, Oregan, Texas, and Utah. The restuarant originated in California and is California's first drive thru burger. It is a family owned restuarant which is known for their not so secret menu, their fresh food, and diner theme. They also have some small details such as bible verses on their wrappers, having their own merchandise, and having palm trees around their restuarents. It is a very popular restuarant that many people love and residents from other states come to taste. In conclusion, their quality and simplicity make In-N-Out a restuarant loved by many.

The Secret Menu

Next is about In-N-Out's famous, not so secret menu. Their secret menu is called the "not so secret menu" because just as their name says, everybody knows about it.
